Super HL FRP Water Tank Descriptions:

  1. FRP is the abbreviation of fiberglass reinforcement plastics,widely used in firefighting,residence and somewhere lacking water.
  2. Fiberglass water tank is assembled by pieces of panels and shape customized according to requirement and some are partitions in the between for better use.
  3. FRP water tank can holds sea water,waste water,drinking water and many kind of water,all panels are made of food grade materials.
  4. The cost is much more performance effective and generally it can use last for over 15 years with easily installation.
  5. FRP water tank has developed more than 20 years and have a mature manufacturing technology and systematic installation instruction(Free video support).

Super HL FRP Water Tank Features:

  1. All panels are made of food grade class materials and national standards.
  2. Inner accessories and outer accessories are alternative for drinking water or non-drinking water.
  3. Thickness tolerance is almost same with the real mark,the below table will show our thickness and corresponding kilograms.
  4. Tank panels are made of three types including 1000x1000mm,1000x500mm,500x500mm,better satisfy the different shape requirement.
  5. U-steel structure is available for both bolt connection and welding connection,material is available for both painted red and hot dip galvanized.
