*Note: This stainless steel hot water tank is our real project in Brunei,do not copy.

Super HL Water Tank Descriptions:

  1. Stainless steel water tank consists of three types of panels which can customize capacity according local area.
  2. The water tank is usually set within 4 meters high,we have set the water tank height up to 6 meters and 5 years from now.
  3. Stainless steel water tank is easily loaded for it light weight and generally packed in plywood case or pre-installation loading as requirement.
  4. Stainless steel water tank warranty time is 18 months since shipped out factory or 12 months since installation,based on close one.
  5. Stainless steel water tank can holds many kinds of water,specially for high quality water,but always compromised to sea water or water with rich chloride.

Super HL Stainless steel water tank features:

  1. All stainless steel materials and national standard.
  2. Panels consist of 1000x1000mm,1000x500mm,500x500mm three types.
  3. Water tank uses the different panel thickness according to above table.
  4. U-steel is hot dip galvanized and painted into red color.
  5. It is alternative to be insulated or not as choices
